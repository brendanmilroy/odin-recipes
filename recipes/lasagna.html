Lasagna

Description

This is just a simple lasagna recipe. Might not be any good, this is all made up.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles until al dente
  2. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta until all pasta is used
  3. Add mozarella to the top
  4. Cook in oven for 30 minutes at 400F
