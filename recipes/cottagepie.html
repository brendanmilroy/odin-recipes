Cottage Pie

Description

This recipe is a classic Quebec recipe known as "Pat√© Chinois". It is an incredibly simple recipe even beginner cooks make it.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Ground beef
  • Cream corn
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Wash, Peel and add the potatoes to boiling water
  2. Add oil to a hot pan and cook the ground beef. Season to taste and set aside.
  3. The potatoes should be ready, drain and mash them with butter and milk
  4. Layer the cooked ingredients: beef, corn, potatoes
  5. Optionally, add grated cheese to the top of the dish before cooking
  6. Cook for 45 minutes at 400F
