Mac and Cheese

Description

This is the perfect recipe for when you are craving mac and cheese. The taste is a little different from the little blue box but it really does taste great while only needing three ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Evaporated milk
  • Grated cheddar

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni in minimal water so that the water evaporates as the noodles are cooked
  2. Add the evaporated milk and stir
  3. Add the cheddar and stir
